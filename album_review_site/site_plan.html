Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to review some of my favorite albums in order to show off the albums to the reader. This will let them make a choice on wether or not they would like to listen to the album or not.

Audience

The audience for this is anyone that is a fan of music and would like to find a new album to listen to.

Content

Home page

The home page is going to be a general introduction to the website. It will state the album that the website will be reviewing and explain to the audience how I will be reviewing it. I will let them know what genre the album is and that the breakdown will consist of the lyrics in the album, the beats/instrumentals, delivery of the lyrics and the overall feeling of the album.

Images for the Home page

Album Breakdown

This will be the main page for the website. This is going to be where the details on the album are going to be. It will go over the lyrics of the album and what they represent/say first so the audience can decide if they are interested in the lyrical content. Then it will go over the the beats and instruments that are used throughout the album giving the audience an idea on what kind of beats to look forward to. It will then go over the delivery of the lyrics such as a calm and soothing delivery or a harsh and aggressive delivery. Finaly it will go over the overall feeling of the album to let the audience know if they are in for a fun and upbeat experience or more of a serious and deep experience.

Images for the Page 2

Album Summary/rating

This page will be where I give an overall summary of the album and the contents that were presented to the audience. after giving a quick summary of the album it will present the audience with a rating out of 10 and the reasons behind the rating. It will then give a link to the album so that if the audience chooses, they can go and listen to it for themselves.
