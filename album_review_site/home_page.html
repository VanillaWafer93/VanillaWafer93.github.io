An Evening With Silk Sonic

"An Evening With Silk Sonic" is an album relaesed in 2021 by Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak. It is a soul/funk album that immediately caught the attention of the world after the release of the groups first single "Leave the Door Open" which became an instant hit. Today we will be diving deeper into this work of art by going over the lyrics, beats/instrumentals, the delivery of the lyrics and finally the overall feeling of the album. At the end of our deep-dive we will let you know ourt rating out of 10 so that you can know what to look forward too with this album!
