A Roller Coaster of an Album

Overall this album is a fantastic revival of the soul/funk genre. With smooth/energetic beats and soulfull/powerfull singing, this album really transports you to a world where you are the man everyone wants to see. With all of this it is only right to give this album a 10/10.

10/10
